# Product Guidelines

## Design Principles
- **Professional & Functional:** The UI should prioritize data density and clarity. Actions should be efficient and well-documented.
- **Clarity over Fluff:** Avoid unnecessary decorative elements. Focus on presenting information in a readable and actionable format.

## Operational Standards
- **Data Integrity:** Implement immediate feedback and real-time validation across all data entry points to minimize human error.
- **Comprehensive Auditing:** Maintain detailed audit trails for modifications to sensitive records, ensuring accountability for financial and client data.
- **Swiss Standards Compliance:** All invoicing and financial features must strictly adhere to Swiss financial regulations and QR billing standards.

## Communication Style
- **Clear & Direct:** System notifications and messages should be concise and professional.
- **Informative Errors:** Error messages must provide actionable information or clear explanations of the validation failure.
- **Language:** Speak in french with me and in the application.
