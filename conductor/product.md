# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
Administrative management system for handling clients, contacts, invoicing, and service provisions.

## Vision
To provide a robust, automated platform for managing complex client engagements and financial workflows, specifically tailored for Swiss invoicing standards and project-based service provisions.

## Primary Goals
- **Client Relationship Management:** Centralize client and contact information to streamline communication and data management.
- **Invoicing Automation:** Automate the creation and processing of Swiss QR bills to ensure financial accuracy and efficiency.
- **Provision & Engagement Tracking:** Efficiently manage and track service provisions and engagements across various editions and project phases.

## Target Users
- **Administrative Staff:** Primary users for billing, data entry, and day-to-day client management tasks.
- **Project Managers:** Users who oversee service delivery, track provisions, and manage high-level engagement status.
